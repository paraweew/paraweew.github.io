Hi there! I am Parawee.

I am a recent graduate from a Master of Finance, University of Tasmania. I have one year of work experience as an actuary in a life insurance company and two months of internship experience in financial services.

My technical skills include advanced Excel, SQL, and R. My passions include mathematics, statistics, and modelling, which I have studied both in my degrees and my spare time. I have completed a number of actuarial exams, for example FM – financial mathematics and MFE – models for financial economics.


I have a “can do” attitude and a passion to challenge myself. I am familiar with working in a diverse environment as I, during my actuarial employment, regularly collaborated with partner companies from Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, and Singapore.

I have always been enthusiastic about extra curriculars. For example, I debated and won a round at the world scholar’s cup in Malaysia. Another highlight was performing a Traditional Thai Dance for Her Royal Highness Princess Soamsawali. Beyond work, I like to volunteer. This has included mentoring science and math in rural parts of Thailand, mentoring new students at Curtin University, and serving in an orphanage. I also have many hobbies, including Taek Won Do, Muay Thai, dancing, and cooking.
